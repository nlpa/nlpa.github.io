About Me

Hello! My name is Natalie B. Lampa. I am a graduate of the University of Michigan with a BS in Information (UX Design) and BA in Asian Studies. I am passionate about people oriented technology and designing with accessibility in mind.

While at UMSI, I have developed strong UX design and analytical skills by taking courses in web design, HCI, and building interactive web apps. Outside of academics, I work as a UX Researcher for IntelleXt Inc. and a UI/UX & Interaction Design intern for Children and Screens: Institute of Digital Media and Child Development.
