About Me

Hi! I'm Natalie. I'm a senior at the University of Michigan pursuing a dual degree between UMSI and LSA in Information (specializing in UX Design and Web Dev) and Southeast Asian Studies. I'm interested in web design, web development, and photography. I am comfortable using HTML/CSS, Python/JavaScript, Figma, and Adobe Photoshop CS6.

Outside of academics, I work as a Research Analyst and Developer for the Knowledge Grid team at Michigan Medicine and as a COVID Support Team Representative at NorthShore University HealthSystem (Evanston). I'm an active member of the Filipino American Student Association at UM and of KickHouse Chicago for kickboxing!
